PunkMap is a verified digital directory that enables CryptoPunks holders to register, browse other profiles, and connect with fellow collectors.

## Features

- **Verified Profiles** - Token-gated authentication ensures only real punk holders can join
- **Privacy-First** - No location sharing required
- **Community Building** - Connect with other CryptoPunks holders worldwide
- **Profile Discovery** - Browse and explore the punk community

A secure way to find and connect with your fellow punks.
