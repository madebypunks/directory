Known as "Non-Fungible Tony" - one of the most significant OG NFT collectors in the space. Minted 100 CryptoPunks in 2017.

Co-founder of NeonDAO, ReadyPlayerDAO, and PaisanoDAO. Co-creator of Ether Cards. Massive contributor to NFT photography and a champion of diversity in the space. Known for onboarding countless people into NFTs by setting up their wallets and gifting them crypto and ENS domains.

"I don't think you can talk about NFTs without CryptoPunks."
