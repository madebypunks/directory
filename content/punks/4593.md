Digital artist Mike Winkelmann, known as Beeple, is one of the most influential figures in digital art. His "Everydays: The First 5000 Days" NFT sold at Christie's for $69 million in March 2021, marking a watershed moment for crypto art.

Creates a new artwork every single day since May 2007. His work blends dystopian imagery, pop culture references, and political commentary.

Contributor to the CryptoPunks: Free to Claim book.
