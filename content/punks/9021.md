Georg Bak, known as SNOWCASH, is a Swiss curator and digital art historian exploring the history of digital art and cyberpunk culture. Co-founder and Artistic Director of Digital Art Mile, an annual exhibition that transforms Basel's historic Rebgasse district into a digital art fair during Art Basel week. He co-curated the SNOWCA$H touring exhibition at Kunsthalle Zürich (2022), named after Neal Stephenson's cyberpunk novel "Snow Crash," bridging physical gallery spaces and multiple metaverses. Also organized NFT Art Day ZRH at Kunsthaus Zürich and curates exhibitions featuring CryptoPunks, Meebits, and other pioneering NFT collections.
