Photographer and mixed-media artist based in New York and Los Angeles. Creator of Twin Flames, a portrait series of 100 pairs of twins from around the world - a project born from the loss of his own twin at birth.

Twin Flames became one of the most iconic NFT photography collections, with #49 selling for $3.78M and #83 becoming the first NFT photograph sold at Christie's ($1.1M). Co-founder of Quantum Art and founder of SaveArtSpace, a non-profit that replaces billboard ads with artwork.
