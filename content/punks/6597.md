Jordan is Chief Product Officer at Art Blocks, where he leads product, engineering, design, and community teams. He brings deep experience building at the intersection of art and technology, having previously founded Prohibition, an early on-chain generative art platform, along with leadership roles at ConsenSys, Nifty's, and the $MEME Project. At Art Blocks, he's guiding the platform's evolution from its foundational AB500 era into mainstream adoption, translating blockchain complexity into seamless experiences that serve both crypto-native collectors and newcomers to digital art.
