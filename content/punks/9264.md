Colombian street artist based in Brooklyn. Creator of the famous "NFT Wall" in Williamsburg - murals of CryptoPunks and Bored Apes commissioned by their holders. Founder of "Got Bricks" and "Another Flex on the Wall". Pioneer in bridging graffiti with the blockchain.
