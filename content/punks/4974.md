Artist Ryan Wilson, known as ThankYouX, creates modern abstract works blurring the line between digital and physical art. His signature cubes and hybrid paintings with embedded screens have captivated collectors worldwide.

Featured in a solo exhibition "inertia" at Sotheby's Beverly Hills in 2023. Part of the Art of Punk exhibition, a museum group show curated by Tschuuuly featuring CryptoPunks holders.
