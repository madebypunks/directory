Ilan Derech (b. 1990, Mexico) is a multidisciplinary cross-chain artist based in Tokyo. Combines street photography and cinematography to create emotionally charged, cinematic scenes. ZEISS Camera lenses ambassador and Leica collaborator, published by National Geographic and Silvergrain Magazine. Creator of "Wabi-Sabi Syntax" - a series of 88 unique NFT artworks inspired by Japanese aesthetics.
