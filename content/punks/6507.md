Sergio Silva, known as Sergito, is a former Goldman Sachs and Barclays professional turned NFT collector and thought leader. CEO of MeebCo, which acquired the Meebits collection from Yuga Labs in February 2025.

His NFT journey started in February 2021 after reading about gmoney's CryptoPunk purchase. "72 hours later, I was completely NFT-pilled." Founding member of NeonDAO, he's become one of the most recognized voices in the NFT space.
