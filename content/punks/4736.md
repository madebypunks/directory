Author, artist, activist, and cultural alchemist. Co-editor of the Phaidon-published "CryptoPunks: Free To Claim" book. Co-founder of Safecast, an environmental non-profit created after the 2011 Fukushima disaster that has collected the world's largest dataset of background radiation measurements. Associate Professor at Keio University and researcher at MIT Media Lab. Background includes putting out punk rock records, co-founding one of the first hackerspaces in the US, and setting up a global blogging network.
