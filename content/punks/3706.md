---
name: CryptoNovo
---

CryptoNovo is a beloved member of the CryptoPunks community who famously recovered his punk #3706 after losing it in a devastating scam on January 4th, 2023. The community rallied around him through the SaveNovo initiative, ultimately helping him buy back his iconic punk.

Known for his dedication to the punk community, Novo is famous for dressing as his punk at events, bringing his digital identity into the physical world in the most authentic way possible.
