Vienna-born, US-based photographer. Clients include ESPN, The Hollywood Reporter, L'Uomo Vogue, Billboard, The New York Times Magazine, and Los Angeles Magazine.

Created the "52icons" NFT collection dedicated to his father Heinz Hosch, a war journalist who passed away at 52. Also created "iCONS of NFT" portrait series with Mike Hager. Official photographer for the Art of Punk exhibition in Linz, Austria.
