General Manager of CryptoPunks, first at Yuga Labs and now consulting for the NODE Foundation. Curated the eight CryptoPunks added to MoMA's permanent collection. Holds degrees in English Literature & Art History from University of Virginia and Literature from University of Colorado Boulder. Background in marketing, event planning, and design at Google, Venables Bell and Partners, and MKTG INC. Leads the iconic collection that ignited a contemporary digital art movement, with pieces in Centre Pompidou, LACMA, and ICA Miami.
