Cambodian-American artist based in Florida. Her colorful, detailed illustrations are inspired by her dreams and her family's experiences as survivors of the Khmer Rouge genocide.

Founder of House of XTAL. Has collaborated with Gucci, Snoop Dogg, Red Bull Racing, and Ubisoft. Featured in Art of Punk, the first museum group show of CryptoPunks holder artists, curated by Tschuuuly.
