French multidisciplinary digital artist known for his Pixel Field concept using pixel drawing, color cycling, and algorithmic processes. Head of Arts and senior curator at Artcrush Gallery.

Work in the Francisco Carolinum museum collection in Linz. Featured in Art of Punk exhibition. Solo shows at NFT Factory Paris and IHAM Gallery. Founder of French Artists on Chain. Curator of digital art for 10,000+ public billboards across 15+ countries.
